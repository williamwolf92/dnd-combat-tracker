Welcome to DnD Combat Tracker. Here's a quick guide:


⚔️ ADDING A COMBATANT

• Go to ⚔️ Combat tab and tap ✦ Add to add Players and Monsters. Enter Name and select a suggestion if you're adding a monster, in this case, Initiative, HP and AC fill automatically.

• Initiative accepts a fixed number (e.g. 14) or a bonus modifier (e.g. -2/+3). A modifier will apply the selected Initiative Rule and add it automatically along with Advan./Disav. if applied.

• HP accepts a fixed number, a roll (#d#) or a roll with bonus (#d#±#).

• AC only accepts a fixed number.

❤️ MODIFYING HP

• Tap the ❤️HP value on a card. Use the numpad to enter a fixed number, a roll (#d#) or a roll with bonus (#d#±#), then tap Damage or Heal.

• Use Resistant or Vulnerable options to halve or duplicate damage respectively. In addition, you can use Resistant in order to halve damage on a successful saving throw.

• A combatant's HP pulses red when it drops to 20% or below.

🛡 ATTACK ROLLS

• Tap the 🛡AC value on a card to open the Attack Roll panel. Set Bonus and Attack Type. The result will show HIT, MISS or CRITICAL!.

• In the combatant card, use the +/− buttons to temporarily adjust AC.

⚡ CONDITIONS

• Tap Cond. on a card to add conditions. Tap a condition chip to remove it.

🧿 FOCUS (Concentration)

• Tap Focus chip on a card to toggle Concentration on a combatant.

• When a focused combatant takes damage, a panel opens automatically: set the DC, the Ability Mod, choose Advantage or Disadvantage if needed and then, tap Roll Save. On failure, Focus is lost.

☠️ DEATH SAVING THROWS

• When a combatant's HP reaches 0, they fall Unconscious and must make Death Saving Throws each turn.

• Tap the ❤️ HP on an active (in-turn) combatant at 0 HP to open the Death Save panel. Choose Advan./Disadv. if applicable, then tap ROLL.

• A roll of 10 or higher counts as a Success; a roll of 9 or lower counts as a Failure. A Natural 1 adds 2 failures. A Natural 20 immediately revives the combatant with 1 HP.

• With 3 Successes, the combatant stabilizes and revives with 1 HP. With 3 Failures, the combatant dies permanently (☠️).

• You can also track saves manually by tapping the ❤️ and 🖤 chips on the combatant card.

💾 IMPORT/EXPORT

• On the Combat screen, use the 💾 / 📁 buttons to export/import the current combat state.

• ⚠️WARNING! Importing combat data will replace ALL the current session as well as the events history.
