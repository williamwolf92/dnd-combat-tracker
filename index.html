Welcome to DnD Combat Tracker. Here's a quick guide:


⚔️ ADDING A COMBATANT

• Go to the Combat tab and tap ✦ Add to add Players and Monsters. Enter Name, Initiative, HP and AC.

• Initiative accepts a fixed number (e.g. 14) or a bonus modifier (e.g. -2 / +3). A modifier will roll 1d20 and add it automatically.

• HP accepts a fixed number or a dice roll in the format #d# or #d#±# (e.g. 8d6+4).

• AC only accepts a fixed number.

❤️ MODIFYING HP

• Tap the ❤️HP value on a card. Use the numpad to enter a fixed number, a roll (#d#) or a roll with bonus (#d#±#), then tap Damage or Heal.

• A combatant's HP pulses red when it drops to 20% or below.

🛡 ATTACK ROLLS

• Tap the 🛡AC value on a card to open the Attack Roll panel. The result will show HIT, FAIL or CRITICAL!.

• Use the +/− buttons to temporarily adjust AC.

⚡ CONDITIONS

• Tap Cond. on a card to add conditions. Tap a condition chip to remove it.

• D.o.T. is a Damage over Time tag — it is not a real D&D condition.
